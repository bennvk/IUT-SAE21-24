About RAMBA

RAMBA is a shop specialized in innovative and quality electronic accessories. Our mission is to offer you reliable, practical, and stylish products to make your daily life easier. We carefully select each item to ensure your satisfaction.

Our team is passionate about technology and always attentive to your needs. Feel free to contact us for any questions or suggestions!

Creation of our company

We are a team of students: Raphaël, Alexis, Mathéo, Benjamin, and Alan. Together, we founded RAMBA, whose name is the acronym of our first names.

As part of the SAE Pépinière, we were tasked with designing, deploying, and securing the entire IT and telephony infrastructure of our company, hosted in a business incubator. Our mission included configuring the network, installing and virtualizing servers, connecting client workstations with different operating systems, setting up IP telephony, ensuring secure communications, managing video surveillance and server temperature monitoring, while facilitating resource sharing and collaboration between our various departments.

This project allowed us to work as a team and develop our skills in network administration, project management, IT security, and web service development, in an environment close to real professional conditions.
